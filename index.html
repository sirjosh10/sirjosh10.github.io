    About This Page

    This is a running list of my Transformers OCs, since I have too many of them. Info is liable to change as the characters become more developed, and the page will more than likely be perpetually under construction.

    That said, feel free to peruse and enjoy!

    Arcane

    Designation: Arcane

    Homeverse: Uniend 911.05 Alpha (Transformers Prime)

    Homeworld: Cybertron

    Age: Unknown

    Status: Deceased

    Pronouns: He/his

    Alt Mode: Cybertronian heavy armor groundcraft

    Faction: Decepticon

    Function: Soldier

    Height: 27'

    Optic/Eye color: Red

    Description: Arcane avoided the war when it first began. He was spurred into action only once his bondmate was killed. He joined the Decepticons, believing both in their ideals of a caste free Cybertron, and that they could protect his creation. Unfortunately, he was unaware that the Decepticons are the very reason his bondmate died.

    Arcane had the ability to manipulate magnetic fields, which is why he was so big in height and bulk alike. (For anchoring purposes) It also made him a target for the enemy, since his ability was a tactical advantage. This, along with his weakened spark (sparkbreak from the extinguishing of a bond) led to his demise.

    Chris

    Designation: Elizabeth "Chris" Christina White

    Homeverse: Uniend 911.05 Alpha (Transformers Prime)

    Homeworld: Earth

    Age: 19 years

    Status: Active

    Pronouns: Female

    Faction: Not aligned

    Height: 5' 5"

    Optic/Eye color: Hazel

    Hair color: Light brown

    Description: Chris has had a less than ideal life. Firstly, she was born to a single mother. Her dad had run out on his pregnant girlfriends, so her mom was forced to raise Chris alone. Since her mom had to drop out of high school, she lacked education. Balancing three jobs, she barely kept the two afloat. This meant the Chris spent most of her childhood alone, as her mom was away working. Child services took her when she was nine, and told her she was in a neglectful home. She begged to differ.

    Chris was bounced around a few foster homes, never fitting in with any of the families, until the day the system dumped her sorry ass on the curb.

    Since then, Chris has always had problems with people, especially males. Once a man had the misfortune of choosing Chris as the girl to harass. He goosed Chris, and she retailiated by breaking his fingers.

    All of them.

    Jobs have been hard to come by since that day, so she's stuck at her minimum wage job at Plus Mart, just to make rent.

    Or she was, until a giant alien death robot all but landed in her lap.

    Circuit

    Designation: Circuit

    Homeverse: Primax 984.17 Alpha (Generation 1)

    Homeworld: Cybertron

    Age: Unknown, but an approximate equivalent to a 20 year old human

    Status: Active

    Pronouns: He/his

    Alt Mode: Nokia Lumia 521 smartphone

    Faction: Not aligned

    Function: Engineer

    Height: 0' 6"

    Optic/Eye color: Purple

    Holoform height: 5' 2"

    Hair color (Holoform): Black

    Description: Circuit fled the war after only a few vorns of existence. He was a commissioned, pre-programmed spark. Someone needed an engineer, though not one so small. However, microformer engineers are quite useful as well. Circuit lived most of his life on Cybertron holed up in a wall, repairing, maintaining, or installing wiring, curcuits, and any other doodad he was told to.

    Once he fled Cybertron, he bounced around from planet to planet and surviving on a hand to mouth basis. (He'd even been known to have to siphon energon from his own lines just to get his ship to limp its way to the next planet.) He ended up crashing on earth, of course the one planet that had not only Autobots AND Decepticons, but THE FACTION LEADERS THEMSELVES.

    He's now staying at the Ark, since his ship was destroyed shortly after his arrival.

    Flair

    Designation: Flair

    Homeverse: Primax 984.17 Alpha (Generation 1)

    Homeworld: Earth

    Age: 5 years

    Status: Active

    Pronouns: She/her

    Alt Mode: iPod Touch

    Faction: Autobot

    Function: Spy

    Height: 0' 5"

    Optic/Eye color: Blue

    Holoform height: 5' 0"

    Hair color (Holoform): Black

    Description: Created by Wheeljack on accident, she posesses a pseudo spark that baffles the entire Autobot science division. Her spark casing had to be built around it AFTER it was created, so her internals look a bit odd. It seems to act like a spark in most ways, though she never reads quite right on scanners and her EM field looks dampened even when she flares it on purpose. Her less than usual creation has never bothered her, as she is too busy trying to make the most of her life. She has the mindset of a human child (an eight year old or so), yet her frame looks like that of a (albeit tiny) femme in her first adult frame.

    She wants nothing more than to help her family, the Autobots, and is often diappointed when told to remain at base for missions. She has gone on some infiltration runs, her diminutive stature making passing through security a much simpler task. This is one of the few times that she acts more befitting of her apparent age, and all childishness drops. The few mechs who have seen her in this state were quite shocked at her maturity and level-headedness.

    As much as she enjoys helping, she still prefers being able to let herself have fun. She enjoys Earth in general, human and Cybertronian culture, animals (the fluffier the better), and meeting new people.

    Polaris

    Designation: Polaris

    Homeverse: Primax 984.17 Alpha (Generation 1)

    Homeworld: Cybertron

    Age: Unknown, approximate equivalent to a 16 year old human

    Status: Active

    Pronouns: She/her

    Alt Mode: Volkswagon Beetle

    Faction: Autobot

    Function: Mobile artillery unit

    Height: 9' 7"

    Optic/Eye color: Blue

    Holoform height: 5' 1"

    Hair color (Holoform): Strawberry blonde

    Description: Polaris knew Orion Pax, back when he was a dock worker. That connection took a good long while to be discovered, Polaris had been on earth for about a week before Optimus realized why she sounded so familiar. Since the discovery, Polaris has treated Optimus like she used to treat Orion. She's been repeatedly seen hugging the Prime, and once even wrapped a feather boa she'd made around his neck. (She stole it back later, but was delighted to find that Optimus hadn't taken it off.)

    She and her girlfriend, Spectrum, landed on Earth after stumbling across a stray Autobot signal (Actually Cosmos contacting base). The Ark crew was obviously surprised, but quite welcoming of the new arrivals.

    Polaris is no frontliner, but she loves the look on a Con's face when the tiny femme they so obviously think is an easy target whips out a cannon bigger than she is. A few bots have actually been made a bit unnerved at the smug smile she wears as she sends a barrage of rockets on whatever unfortunate Decepticon has crossed paths with her.

    Quartzite

    Designation: Quartzite

    Homeverse: Primax 984.17 Alpha (Generation 1)

    Homeworld: Cybertron

    Age: Unknown, approximate equivalent to a 50 year old human

    Status: Active

    Pronouns: She/her

    Alt Mode: Cybertronian microscope/laser cutter

    Faction: Not aligned

    Function: Shopkeeper

    Height: 1" 9"

    Optic/Eye color: Yellow

    Description: Quartzite defied the stereotypical expectations of someone her frame type. She kept her shop (a combination of a repair store, pawn shop, and antique restoring) running, though she was always far from wealthy. Her customers were loyal, and that was what kept her afloat. She lived in a small room hidden behind the front counter of the shop, more of a glorified broom closet than a respectable berth room. She didn't care, she loved her job.

    The only thing she cared about more than her precious shop was her creation, Vanadia. When war broke out, they were slow to leave. Eventually they did, fleeing to a hub of a planet that brought in new clients in droves. She restarted her shop, and it was much more successful than it ever was on Cybertron. Smaller species appreciated her lack of height, while larger species found her size novel.

    Her hands only have four fingers each, and her digits are much thinner than normal so that fine repair work can be done without them getting in the way. (Think just a bit thicker than TFP Soundwave's digits.) They also have the ability to hyperextend for the same reason. They contain many more sensors than the average servo, which makes delicate work easier.

    Vanadia

    Designation: Vanadia

    Homeverse: Primax 984.17 Alpha (Generation 1)

    Homeworld: Cybertron

    Age: Unknown, approximate equivalent to a 19 year old human

    Status: Active

    Pronouns: She/her

    Alt Mode:

    Faction: Not aligned

    Function: Tattoo artist, armor engraver, flavored energon maker

    Height: 2' 7"

    Optic/Eye color: Yellow

    Description: Vanadia worked as an engineer, usually holed up in walls or consoles for repair and maintenence. She was good at her job, but much preferred working in her mom's store.

    She lived in her creator's shop, the door to her room situated by the display of antique engraved energon cylinders in the back of the store. She did very high quality engravings (read as: Cybertronian tattoos) for a very low price. She also had the best flavored energon in Altihex. Sadly, she had to charge a bit more for that, since supplies were expensive. Her customers didn't seem to mind, though.

    When she and her creator, Quartzite, fled Cybertron she restarted her side business alongside her mother's shop. Honestly,she was happy to be free of her engineering duties. The extra time allowed her to perfect her engraving and master tattoos and piercings, which she offered to her non-metallic customers.

    She was built with hands like her mother. In order to do her engineering duties, her mother created a set of armor to put on over her hands, essentially gloves. They thicken her fingers to an average width and prevent hyperextension. They contain the same stimuli detectors in a normal servo, so she feels less through them than with her own hyper-sensitive servos. When the gloves are off the data port for those sensors to plug into are visible in her wrists. Whenever she isn't at work she has them off because likes the fact that she shares a feature with her creator, and she misses the tactile sensations absent while wearing the extra armor.
